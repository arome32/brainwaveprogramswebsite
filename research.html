The Brainwave Programs'
Spring - Summer 2018 Research Project

For Children with Learning Disabilities, Dyslexia or ADD/ADHD


The Brainwave Programs are a powerful and effective series of brain stimulation exercises that improve the scholastic and social abilities of those diagnosed with ADD/ADHD, Dyslexia, Learning Disabilities and Anxiety. These programs achieve rapid results for children and adults without using medication.

CRITERIA FOR ADMISSION TO THE PROJECT
  • 9 - 14 years old, struggling in school
  • Identified ADD/ADHD, Dyslexia, or Learning Disabilities
Overview of the Project
  • An Initial Assessment
  • An Educational Analysis with Printout
  • A Customized Exercise Plan
  • 18 Personal Appointments
  • A Post-Test at the completion of your child's program with printout
HOW TO APPLY
  • Complete the online registration form.
  • You will be notified of your child's acceptance to the project.
  • Upon acceptance, a questionnaire will be emailed to parents/caregivers.
  • Cost: A deposit of $50.00 CDN for materials will be required prior to the commencement of the research project to be returned at your child's last appointment.

Dates: June to August 2018 (weekends)

Location: St. Davids, Ontario (Niagara Region) Directions
