The Brainwave Programs' Education Specialist Courses Levels I-IV©

LEVEL I: MAY 31-JUNE 3, 2019



Course Level I©


"Setting Your Classroom and Private Practice in Motion"

You will learn The Brainwave Programs' Brain Theory and Physiology, Part I
  • The brain's developmental stages, its anatomy and physiology.
  • The brain's health and environmental needs.
  • The causes of A.D.D., dyslexia, depression and anxiety.

You will learn about:
  • The Brainwave Programs' Professional Assessment Process
  • How to take your students'/clients' health, education and family history.
  • A comprehensive educational analysis based on the Gifford model, which tests different intellectual processing abilities and is especially accurate in pinpointing areas that are underdeveloped as well as those that are gifted.
  • How to administer, score and analyze your students'/clients' test data.

LEVEL II: JULY 12-15, 2019



Course Level II©


"Developing Personal Learning Programs and Implementing Strategies"

You will learn The Brainwave Programs' Brain Theory and Physiology, Part II

You will learn how:
  • To determine where help is needed (neurological, physical and educational).
  • To use The Brainwave Programs exercises for improved concentration, memory, communication, reading, spelling, math, comprehension and social interaction skills.
  • To coach participating adults to apply the individual child's program needs.
  • To design your students'/clients' programs to meet the province's/state's curriculum requirements.
  • To enable your students/clients to surpass their learning objectives.

LEVEL III: AUGUST 17-19, 2019



Course Level III©


"Brain Function, Anti-Depressant Activities, and Adult Support Programs"

You will learn The Brainwave Programs' Brain Theory and Physiology, Part III

You will learn how:
  • Past trauma are directly circuited into the neurological system to create old unproductive habits, negative mental states, depression, anxiety and phobias, as well as learning, concentration and memory problems.
  • You will become adept at overriding old neurological loops and witnessing the immediate personal changes that occur with proactive intervention
  • You will study current brain research publications that are at the core of The Brainwave Programs.

LEVEL IV: (GRADUATION 2020) DATE TBA



Course Level IV©


"The Brainwave Programs for Neuro-Emotional Release©
and Recreating Your Professional Space or Classroom"

You will learn The Brainwave Programs' Brain Theory and Physiology, Part IV

For the adult program, you will learn to use The Brainwave Programs for Neuro-Emotional© technique to address clients' personal problems.

You will learn how:
  • To use The Brainwave Programs in the resource room, classroom or in private practice.
  • To learn effective ways to promote yourself
  • You will be taught the legal requirements of managing a private practice.



Course Prerequisites

  1. Professional Education Specialist Training participants will be working in the education or health care fields currently.
  2. A preliminary questionnaire and interview must be completed.
  3. Established practitioners are not expected to participate in the interview process or in parts 2 and 3 of Course IV.
  4. Participants will have taken or will be in the process of taking Brainwave Program 1 or 2.
  5. The times for taking Brainwave Programs 1 or 2 will be arranged according to individual schedules.
  6. For final certification, participants will have taken five clients through either Brainwave Programs 1 or 2.
