Brainwave Program 2©

For Adults with ADD, Learning Disabilities, Anxiety,
Depression or Emotional Discomfort


Participant's time investment over an approximate 6 month period is comprised of:

  • 30 minute, 4 times a week of Home Exercises
  • 11 Office Visits, which include
  • A 4.5 hour initial assessment and educational analysis
  • 3 hour basic training
  • 9 personal appointments
    (to customize and update program exercises according to individual progress)

Complimentary written reports to professional agencies are available upon request
